Theme: For social good.

Reward ideas that create a real, positive impact on people, communities, or the
planet — solving a genuine problem for an underserved or affected group. Favor
solutions that are useful, clearly benefit others (not just the builder), and
could realistically help if they existed. Novelty and demo-ability still matter,
but the core question is: does this make life meaningfully better for someone who
needs it?
